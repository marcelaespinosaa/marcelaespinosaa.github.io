I'm a Senior Product Designer focused on shaping complex systems into intuitive, scalable experiences.

My work sits at the intersection of enterprise systems, workflow automation, and emerging AI interactions, where I enjoy turning messy, undefined problems into structured, usable solutions. I'm particularly drawn to designing experiences that reduce manual effort, support decision-making, and fit naturally into how people already work.

I'm a highly collaborative designer who works closely with product and engineering partners to bring ideas to life. I'm known for strong communication, attention to detail, and creating clear, implementation-ready designs that help teams move quickly without losing quality.

I approach design with a systems mindset — thinking beyond individual screens to ensure consistency, scalability, and long-term impact across the product.

Outside of work, I'm bilingual (Spanish/English), spend time with my dog and cats, and practice pottery as a way to slow down and recharge. I also care deeply about mental health and try to bring empathy and intention into both my work and the teams I'm part of.
